Definitions

Fruit
A fruit is the mature ovary of a flowering plant, usually containing seeds. Fruits are often sweet or sour and eaten as snacks or desserts.
Vegetable
A vegetable is any edible part of a plant that is not a fruit. Vegetables include leaves, stems, roots, and bulbs and are typically savory or mild in flavor.
